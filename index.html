Selected Projects

 Modeling the Growth Potential of the Kelp Saccharina Latissima in the North Atlantic - I worked with Dr John Taylor from the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics, at the University of Cambridge, to use a mathematical model of S. latissima (implemented in Julia) forced by ocean state estimates to determine which areas of the North Atlantic may be suitable for growing kelp (a proposed carbon sequestration strategy).
 SugarKelp.jl : Model of sugar kelp in Julia - developed as part of my summer research project investigating the growth potential of kelp in the North Atlantic (for the purpose of carbon sequestration)
 CamPyRoS : Unguided rocket trajectory simulation in Python- part of the work as Cambridge University Space Flight software, simulation, and modelling project manager
 getgfs : get NOAA GFS forecasts in Python- developed for use in CamPyRoS since alternatives use platform specific dependancies
